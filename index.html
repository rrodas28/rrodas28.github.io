About Rebecca

Have you been having a challenging time? Well, my name is Rebecca, and I am here to support you through the chaotic journey of life. Through talk therapy and art therapy directives, let's explore the challenges you are facing. Let's process emotions, explore identity, learn coping skills, communicate better, set boundaries, and improve your mood.

Contact Me

Qualifications

Rebecca holds an MPS in Art Therapy from School of Visual Arts and is a Licensed Creative Arts Therapist (LCAT). She is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for her clients.

Learn More

Services

Rebecca specializes in art therapy and psychotherapy, providing services for anxiety, depression, and trauma. Her therapeutic approach includes cognitive-behavioral, humanistic, and psychodynamic modalities.
